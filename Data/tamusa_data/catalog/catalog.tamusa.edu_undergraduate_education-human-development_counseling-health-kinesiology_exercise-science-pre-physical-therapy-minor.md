# Exercise Science & Pre-Physical Therapy, Minor
The Exercise Science and Pre-PT minor is for students interested in a non-public school teaching profession, continuing their education in Exercise Science, or applying to a PT program to further their education:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Required Courses  
Basic Physiology of Exercise 1 | 4  
Functional Anatomy 2 | 3  
Biomechanics 1 | 3  
Exercise Test and Prescription 3 | 4  
Foundations of Kinesiology | 3  
Fitness and Wellness  
Select one of the following: | 3-4  
| Exercise in Chronic Disease and Disabilities  
| Adv Physiology of Exercise  
| Motor Dev and Motor Learning  
Total Credits | 20-21 

1 
      
[EDKN 3315](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDKN%203315 "EDKN 3315") Functional Anatomy is a prerequisite. 

2 
    
[BIOL 1306](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=BIOL%201306 "BIOL 1306") Gen Biology I-Attr Living Sys is a prerequisite. 

3 
    
[EDKN 3426](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDKN%203426 "EDKN 3426") Basic Physiology of Exercise is a prerequisite.
