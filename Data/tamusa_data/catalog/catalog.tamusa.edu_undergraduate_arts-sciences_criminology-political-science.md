# Department of Criminology and Political Science
The Department of Criminology and Political Science includes a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) in Criminology and Criminal Justice, a Bachelor of Applied Arts and Sciences (B.A.A.S.) in Criminology and Criminal Justice, and a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) in Political Science. The three programs prepare students to be successful in areas such as the criminal justice system, public and private sector fields such as data analytics, business, and finance. Our immersive learning opportunities provide students with valuable firsthand experience to lead the way as agents of change.
  * [Army Reserve Officer Training Corps (ROTC)](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/rotc/)
  * [Criminology and Criminal Justice Concentration, Bachelor of Applied Arts and Sciences](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/criminology-criminal-justice-concentration-baas/)
  * [Criminology and Criminal Justice, Bachelor of Science](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/criminology-criminal-justice-bs/)
  * [Criminology and Criminal Justice, Minor](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/criminology-criminal-justice-minor/)
  * [Mexican American, Latinx, and Borderlands Studies, Minor](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/mexican-american-latinx-borderlands-studies-minor/)
  * [Military Science, Minor](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/military-science-minor/)
  * [Peace and Conflict Studies, Minor](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/undergraduate/arts-sciences/criminology-political-science/peace-conflict-minor/)
