# Spanish, Minor
To earn a minor in Spanish, a student must complete:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Intermediate Spanish II (or equivalent ) | 3  
Select an additional minimum number of 15 semester hours, 12 of which must be upper-division 1  | 15   
Total Credits | 18       
beyond [SPAN 1311](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=SPAN%201311 "SPAN 1311") Elementary Spanish I, [SPAN 1312](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=SPAN%201312 "SPAN 1312") Elementary Spanish II, and [SPAN 2311](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=SPAN%202311 "SPAN 2311") Intermediate Spanish I
