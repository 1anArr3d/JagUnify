# Entrepreneurship Certificate
The certificate in entrepreneurship will provide students with important entrepreneurial skills, i.e. developing a business idea, developing a business plan, obtain financing, and commercializing the product/service. These are the most desirable skills that entrepreneurs want to learn. 
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Introduction to Entrepreneurship: Exploring the World of the Entrepreneur | 3  
Introduction to the Entrepreneurial Experience-From Ideation to Launch  | 3  
Select 2 of the following courses: | 6  
| Entrp: Small Business Management  
| Entrepreneurship Internship  
| Mastering the Art of Pitching Your Business Ideas  
| Developing Your Business Plan  
| Great Books in Entrepreneurship  
| Scale Up Mastery: Growing Your Business  
| Entrp: Spcl Topics Entrprnrshp  
| Social Entrepreneurship  
| Entrepreneurial Finance  
Total Credits | 12  
_The certificate will only be awarded upon successful completion of degree and all four courses._
