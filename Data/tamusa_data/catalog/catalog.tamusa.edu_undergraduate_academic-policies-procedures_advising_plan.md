# Academic Plan
A student's plan for completing degree requirements should be created in consultation with their assigned academic advisor. Any exceptions to the degree requirements published in this catalog must be approved by the dean of the college in which the major is taken. All undergraduate degrees must conform to all Academic Policies and Procedures as set forth in this section of the catalog.
