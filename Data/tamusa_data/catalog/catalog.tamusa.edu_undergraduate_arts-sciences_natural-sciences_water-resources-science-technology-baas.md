_This suggested plan of study is intended to be used as a guide in conjunction with official degree requirements outlined in the catalog. This plan is based on existing partnerships that allow students to complete their A.A.S. degree before transferring to A &M-San Antonio to complete the upper-division program. Students should regularly consult with academic advisors as they plan their course schedules as course offerings may vary. _
