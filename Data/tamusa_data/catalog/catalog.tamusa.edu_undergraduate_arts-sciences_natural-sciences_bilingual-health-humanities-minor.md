# Bilingual Health Humanities, Minor
## Students will take 18 credit hours, at least 12 are required courses in Spanish. They may choose from the following list of courses.
## **REQUIRED**
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Required Courses | 15  
| Spanish Language Studies IV  
| Conversational Spanish  
| Writing for Wide Audiences  
| Language, Culture, and Communication in Latina/o/e Health  
| Foundations of Community Health  
Electives - Select 3 hours from the following | 3  
| Health Writing  
| The Science of Health Disparities  
| Introduction to Mexican American, Latinx, and Borderlands Studies  
| Death and Dying  
| Health and Society  
| Borderland Rhetorics  
| Biomedical Ethics  
Total Credits | 18
