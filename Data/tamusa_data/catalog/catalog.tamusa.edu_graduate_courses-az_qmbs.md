# Quantitative Methods and Business Statistics (QMBS)
**QMBS 5337** **Managerial Bus Statistics** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Selected statistical methods involving quality control, forecasting, sampling and other business applications using computer based approaches.
**Restrictions:** Undergraduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students in a Master of Business Admin degree.
