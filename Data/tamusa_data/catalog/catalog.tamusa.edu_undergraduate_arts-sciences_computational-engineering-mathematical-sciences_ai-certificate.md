# A.I. Certificate
With the growth of student enrollment in computing programs and the demand for leveraging AI tools and applications, the AI Certificate is designed to provide computing majors with the essential knowledge and practical skills needed for the AI workforce.
**Undergraduate AI Certificate requires completion of the following courses:**
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Artificial Intelligence | 3  
Big Data Systems | 3  
Machine Learning | 3
