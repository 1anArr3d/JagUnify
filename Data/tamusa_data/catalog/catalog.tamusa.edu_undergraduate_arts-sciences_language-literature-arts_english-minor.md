# English, Minor
To earn a minor in English, a student must complete:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Intro to English Studies | 3  
Select a minimum of 15 additional semester hours in English at least 12 of which must be upper-division 1  | 15   
Total Credits | 18       
beyond [ENGL 1301](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=ENGL%201301 "ENGL 1301") Composition I and [ENGL 1302](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=ENGL%201302 "ENGL 1302") Composition II
