# Educator CAFE
The Educator CAFE oversees the Certification, Assessment, and Field Experiences aspects of the Educator Preparation Program (EPP) in the College of Education & Human Development. The Educator CAFE faculty are committed to supporting the development of undergraduate and graduate certification candidates through intentional preparation and research-based professional development.
