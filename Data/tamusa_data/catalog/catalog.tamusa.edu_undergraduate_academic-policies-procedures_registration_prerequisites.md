# Prerequisites
No student shall be allowed any credits for a course before credit in its prerequisite is obtained, except on the written approval of the Department Chair of the program offering the course. Also, the course(s) to be taken in lieu of the prerequisite(s) must be approved in writing by the Department Chair of the program in the student’s major before the prerequisite course(s) requirement may be waived.
All students must be complete in all three areas of TSI (Math, Reading, and Writing) in order to register in upper division courses (3000- and 4000-level). If the student is not complete in all three areas, they will not be eligible to register in upper division courses.
