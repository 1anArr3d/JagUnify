# Business Law (BLAW)
**BLAW 2341** **Business Law** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
This course introduces the nature and structure of the US and Texas legal systems; the basic principles of legal reasoning; and laws having as a substantial impact on business including contracts, business organizations, and the Uniform Commercial Code. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [ENGL 1301](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=ENGL%201301 "ENGL 1301") and [ENGL 1302](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=ENGL%201302 "ENGL 1302").
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**BLAW 4344** **International Business Law** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
This course introduces comparative law; the nature and structure of international law; international agreement governing dispute resolution, trade, the sale of goods, and direct foreign investment; and the legal risks of international business. 
**Prerequisites:** BLAW 3341 TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
