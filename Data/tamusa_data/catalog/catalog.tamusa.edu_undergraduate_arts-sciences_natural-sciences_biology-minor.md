# Biology, Minor
To earn a minor in Biology, students are advised to take [BIOL 1306](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=BIOL%201306 "BIOL 1306") Gen Biology I-Attr Living Sys and [BIOL 1307](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=BIOL%201307 "BIOL 1307") Gen Biology II-Biol Organisms to fulfill their core requirements in the physical and life sciences in order to stay within 120 credit hours for their degree plans. A minor in biology thus consists of 6 credits in the core (General Biology I and II), and an additional 16-18 credit hours in BIOL. See below for specific courses and course-requirements for a Biology Minor.
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Gen Biology I-Attr Living Sys | 3  
Gen Biology II-Biol Organisms | 3  
General Biology I Lab | 1  
General Biology II Lab | 1  
Genetics | 4  
Select an additional 10-12 credits in Biology 1  | 10-12  
Total Credits | 22-24
