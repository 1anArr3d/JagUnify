# Special Education, Bachelor of Science
The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) in Special Education is designed to help future teachers create an environment in the classroom that supports children’s growth and learning, develop inclusive instructional plans reflecting evidence-based practices, and generate learning opportunities that meet the needs of diverse students based on the student's individual educational plan. The degree focuses on practical application, pedagogical practices, and assessment to prepare high-quality teachers to enter the field of special education.
