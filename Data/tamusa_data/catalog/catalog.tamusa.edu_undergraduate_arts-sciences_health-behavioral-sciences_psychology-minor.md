# Psychology, Minor
The minor consists of a total of 19-20 hours, including:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
General Psychology 1  | 3  
Psychological Research/Stats I | 3  
Psychological Research/Stats 1 Lab | 1  
Select at least 12-13 hours of upper-division Psychology courses 2 | 12-13  
Total Credits | 19-20       
A minimum grade of C in [PSYC 2301](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=PSYC%202301) General Psychology​ is required of students pursuing a minor in Psychology.     
If students choose to take PSYC 4310, PSYC 4328, or PSYC 3304 for the upper division electives, the corresponding lab will also be required.
