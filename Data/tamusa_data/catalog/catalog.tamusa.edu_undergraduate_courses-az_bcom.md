# Business Communications (BCOM)
**BCOM 3304** **Bus Communication** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Students investigate best practices in business communication by writing effective letters, memos, emails, and reports. They learn the importance of oral communication and practice delivering presentations. During the course, students explore how to use artificial intelligence responsibly to improve their research and writing skills. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, Writing
**Prerequisites:** [ENGL 1301](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=ENGL%201301 "ENGL 1301") and [ENGL 1302](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=ENGL%201302 "ENGL 1302").
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
