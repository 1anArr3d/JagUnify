# Mathematics, Minor
The minimum requirement to earn a minor in Mathematics is 18 hours to include:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
[MATH 2313](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=MATH%202313 "MATH 2313") | Calculus Iand Calculus I Lab | 4  
[MATH 2314](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=MATH%202314 "MATH 2314") | Calculus IIand Calculus II Lab | 4  
Select 12 additional hours of upper-division math electives  1  | 12   
Total Credits | 20 

1 
      
excluding [MATH 3350](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=MATH%203350 "MATH 3350") Probability & Stats for Tchrs, [MATH 3301](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=MATH%203301 "MATH 3301") Biostatistics (Formerly MATH 4301), and [MATH 3360](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=MATH%203360 "MATH 3360") Modern Geometry
