# Biochemistry, Minor
The following courses are required:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
General Chemistry I | 3  
General Chemistry Lab I | 1  
General Chemistry II | 3  
General Chemistry Lab II | 1  
Organic Chemistry I | 3  
Organic Chemistry I Lab | 1  
Organic Chemistry II | 3  
Organic Chemistry II Lab | 1  
Biochemistry I | 3  
Biochemistry Laboratory | 1  
Biochemistry II | 3  
If your major degree plan requires any of the above courses, in order to declare biochemistry as a minor, you must have 12 credit hours unique to the minor.  
Total Credits | 23
