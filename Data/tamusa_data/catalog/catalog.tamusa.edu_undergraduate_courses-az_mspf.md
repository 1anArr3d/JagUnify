# Military Sci Physical Fitness (MSPF)
**MSPF 3131** **Military Physical Fitness** **Credit:** 1 (1-0-0)
This course is designed to provide ROTC students with an introduction to physical fitness training in preparation for a military career, improve quality of life, as well as developing one's physical and mental health. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
