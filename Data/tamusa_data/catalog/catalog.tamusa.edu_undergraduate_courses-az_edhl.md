# Health (EDHL)
**EDHL 3333** **Growth Dvlpmnt and Sexuality** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Human sexuality content for different developmental stages. Designed for individuals who plan to present human sexuality content to various age groups. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**EDHL 3335** **Drug Education** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Study of prescription and non-prescription drugs, their action in the body, their benefits and abuse potentials. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**EDHL 3381** **Community Health** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Acquaints the student with all aspects of community and public health. Designed to prepare students for planning, implementation and evaluation of health education/health promotion programs in community, health care and worksite settings. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**EDHL 3383** **Women's Health** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Medical, historical, legal, environmental, sociological and psychological issues that affect women’s health, both past and present. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDKN 1304](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDKN%201304 "EDKN 1304").
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**EDHL 4337** **Disease Entities/Epidemiology** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
This course introduces epidemiologic principles and methods. Examines the historical and theoretical bases of epidemiology; statistical methods; distribution of disease over person, place and time; research methods utilized in epidemiology; and the application of epidemiology to the prevention of disease and the promotion of health. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**EDHL 4342** **Prgrm Plan for Hlth Promotion** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Explore the demographics of the San Antonio area, identify a disease that is prevalent to a local population, assess the resources available, develop a health promotion program and implement the program at a local facility. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDHL 3381](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDHL%203381 "EDHL 3381").
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
