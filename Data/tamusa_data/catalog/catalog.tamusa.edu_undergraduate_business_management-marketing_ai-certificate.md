# A.I. Essentials for Business Certificate
The exponential growth of artificial intelligence is rapidly changing the way for-profit, non-profit, and public sector organizations operateKlein (2023) argues it is imperative for educators to help students become informed consumers of artificial intelligence. “AI literacy is something that every student needs exposure tonot just those who are planning on a career in computer science” (para. 6). The A.I. Essentials for Business Certificate provides students with a basic understanding of how AI works, experiential activities that explore the technology, and opportunities to discuss ethical issues related to AI.
The  _A.I. Essentials for Business Certificate_ provides students from all academic programs with an interdisciplinary opportunity to learn about artificial intelligence in a variety of industries (e.g., healthcare, law enforcement, retail, manufacturing, human resources, agriculture, nonprofits, etc.). The certificate’s courses provide:
  * A hands-on opportunity to work with artificial intelligence.
  * Valuable insight into how AI improves individual and organizational performance.
  * An efficient way for COB students to earn a certificate that provides them with a competitive advantage in the workforce.


To earn the _A.I. Essentials for Business Certificate_ students are required to complete 4 courses related to the strategic application of artificial intelligence.
