# Women’s and Gender Studies, Minor
This minor requires Intro Women's and Gender Stds ([WGST 2301](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=WGST%202301 "WGST 2301")) and 15 additional semester hours of courses from the list below, at least 12 of which must be upper-division. No more than 9 hours in any one discipline may apply toward the Women’s and Gender Studies minor.

Intro Women's and Gender Stds | 3
