# College of Arts and Sciences
## Introduction
The faculty of the College of Arts and Sciences excel in teaching, scholarship, and service. The faculty engage students with a curriculum that promotes learning in a culturally diverse environment and serves the needs of traditional and non-traditional students. In pursuit of our mission, the College challenges our students to apply their intellectual curiosity and skills in order to improve their communities.
