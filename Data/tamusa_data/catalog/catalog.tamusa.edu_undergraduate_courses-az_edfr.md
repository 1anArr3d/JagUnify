# Education Field Residency (EDFR)
**EDFR 0000** **TExES Exam Preparation** **Credits:** 0 (0-0-0)
This course will provide an analysis of each Domain and Standards of the Core test needed to become initially certified. An analysis of the content, format and techniques to master the Core will be used. This test prep course may include other Core areas per student need. 
**Prerequisites:** Admission to EPP or GTCP.
**Restrictions:** Enrollment limited to students in the College of Educ Human Dev college.
**Repeat Status:** Course may be repeated 1 time(s).
**EDFR 3110** **Field-Based Experience I** **Credit:** 1 (0-0-1)
This lab course meets the TEA 50-hour field-based experience requirement and is designed to equip teacher candidates with classroom observation/reflection experiences in the field. Assignments within the course prepare teacher candidates for certification as well as their clinical teaching experience. The course must be successfully completed prior to participation in clinical teaching. Restriction(s): Enrollment is limited to students formally admitted to the teacher preparation program. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** Admission to the teacher preparation program.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students with the Admitted College Education-EPP attribute.
**EDFR 3120** **Field-Based Experience II** **Credit:** 1 (0-0-1)
This lab course requires 50-hours of field-based experience and is designed to equip pre-teacher candidates with classroom observation/reflection/participation in the field. Assignments within the course prepare teacher candidates for certification as well as their clinical teaching experience. The course must be successfully completed prior to participation in clinical teaching. Restriction(s): Enrollment is limited to students formally admitted and in good standing within the teacher preparation program. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDFR 3110](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%203110 "EDFR 3110") and in good standing within the teacher preparation program.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students with the Admitted College Education-EPP attribute.
**EDFR 4310** **Clinical Teach: Teach Res Sem1** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Within this course students satisfy the first semester of Clinical Teacher Residency requirements as well as demonstrate the knowledge and skills necessary for successful teaching. The Teacher Residency program works in conjunction with approved and accredited district partners. Subject-specific portfolio-based assessment training and practice are embedded in this course to prepare students for [EDFR 4320](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%204320 "EDFR 4320") in which they will be required to complete a portfolio-based assessment of their teaching performance. Restriction(s): Passing scores on the core content TExES exam are required for participation in clinical teaching. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDFR 3110](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%203110 "EDFR 3110"), Admitted and in good standing within the teacher preparation program.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students with the Admitted College Education-EPP attribute.
**EDFR 4320** **Clinical Teach: Teach Res Sem2** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Within this course students satisfy the second semester of Clinical Teacher Residency requirements as well as demonstrate the knowledge and skills necessary for successful teaching. The Teacher Residency program works in conjunction with approved and accredited district partners. Students are required to complete and submit a subject-specific portfolio-based assessment of their teaching performance. Restriction(s): Passing scores on the core content TExES exams are required for participation in clinical teaching. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDFR 4310](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%204310 "EDFR 4310"), Admitted and in good standing with the teacher preparation program.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students with the Admitted College Education-EPP attribute.
**EDFR 4333** **Special Topics in Education** **Credits:** 3 (3-0-0)
Major problems and issues in the field of education will be covered. Topics addressed may include online learning design, special needs in the classroom, student assessment, initial teacher certification requirements, equity and inclusion in the classroom, and cultural competence within schools. Course may be repeated once for credit.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
**EDFR 4613** **Elementary Clinical Teaching: Field Residency III** **Credits:** 6 (0-0-6)
Students will demonstrate, in a laboratory setting, knowledge of and an ability to effectively apply those skills necessary for successful teaching in the elementary school. Student teaching is a full-time assignment for 16 weeks in an accredited elementary school. Student is to furnish transportation. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDFR 3110](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%203110 "EDFR 3110"), [EDFR 3120](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%203120 "EDFR 3120"), and all course work on degree plan.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students with the Admitted College Education-EPP attribute.
**EDFR 4623** **Scndry Clinic Tch:Fld Res III** **Credits:** 6 (0-0-6)
Students will demonstrate, in a laboratory setting, knowledge of and an ability to effectively apply those skills necessary for successful teaching in the elementary school. Student teaching is a full-time assignment for 16 weeks in an accredited secondary school. Student is to furnish transportation. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing
**Prerequisites:** [EDFR 3110](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%203110 "EDFR 3110"), [EDFR 3120](https://catalog.tamusa.edu/search/?P=EDFR%203120 "EDFR 3120"), and all course work on degree plan.
**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.Enrollment limited to students with the Admitted College Education-EPP attribute.
**EDFR 4633** **Elem Clinical Teach/Performance** **Credits:** 6 (0-6-0)
Within this course elementary pre-service teachers demonstrate the knowledge and skills necessary for successful teaching in an elementary setting. The teacher preparation program works in conjunction with approved and accredited area districts to obtain placements for clinical teaching. This course includes the creation and submission of a subject specific portfolio-based assessment. Restriction(s): Passing scores on the core content TExES exams are required for participation in clinical teaching. TSI Restriction(s): Reading, Math, and Writing

**Restrictions:** Graduate level students may not enroll.
