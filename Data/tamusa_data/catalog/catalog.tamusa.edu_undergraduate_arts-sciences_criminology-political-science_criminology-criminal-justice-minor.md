# Criminology and Criminal Justice, Minor
To earn a minor in criminology and criminal justice, a student must complete:
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Intro to Criminal Justice | 3  
Theories of Crime Causation | 3  
Crime & Justice Policy | 3  
Criminal Law | 3  
Select six additional hours of upper division CRIM courses | 6  
Total Credits | 18
