# Computer Science, Minor
Available to any major
The computer science minor consists of the following required courses (18 hours):
Course List Code | Title | Credits  
---|---|---  
Programming Fundamentals I | 4  
Programming Fundamentals II | 4  
Programming Fundamentals III | 4  
Database Systems | 3  
Select three additional semester credit hours from an upper-division CSCI course or approved CISA/CETE elective | 3   
Total Credits | 18
