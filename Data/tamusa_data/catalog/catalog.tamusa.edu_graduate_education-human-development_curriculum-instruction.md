# Department of Curriculum and Instruction
## **Mission**
The mission of the Department of Curriculum & Instruction is to serve as the foundation for all education programs across disciplines by supporting the development and use of effective strategies in culturally sustaining pedagogy and assessment.
The department offers:
  * Master of Arts in Curriculum & Instruction - Teaching in Diverse Communities
  * Master of Science in Reading 
    * with option to add the Reading Specialist Certification
  * Reading Specialist Certification (non-degree seeking)
